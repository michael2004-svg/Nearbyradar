Features

Built for
local commerce.

🗺️
Live Google Maps Integration

Real-time shop locations on Google Maps. Accurate pin placement, street view, and turn-by-turn directions built in.

📏
Exact Distance Sorting

Shops sorted by walking and driving distance from where you are right now. Closest first, always.

🔍
Product-Level Search

Don't just find shops — find which specific nearby shop has the exact product you need. Search by product name.

📱
Shop Profiles

Every shop gets a full profile: name, category, products, hours, photos, and contact details. One tap to call.

Shop Owner Analytics

Shop owners see how many people viewed their listing, searched for them, or clicked directions. Know your reach.

Customers leave ratings and short reviews. Shops with great service rise to the top of search results.

New Shop Alerts

Get notified when a new shop opens near you in a category you care about. Never miss a new local option.

Inventory Hints

Shops can mark items as in stock, low stock, or out of stock so customers don't make wasted trips.
